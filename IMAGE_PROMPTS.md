## Tips for Best Results on Freepik

- **Style:** Always select "Photography" > "Realistic" or "Photorealistic"
- **Ratio:** Use 16:9 or 3:2 landscape for best card fit
- **Lighting:** The prompts already include lighting cues — don't remove them
- **Quality:** Download at highest resolution available (usually 2x or 4x)
- **File format:** Save as `.jpg` (not .webp or .png) for faster loading
